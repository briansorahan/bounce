March 2025 Announcement

Introducing Bounce

Bounce is an experimental audio editor focused on corpus analysis and resynthesis. Driven by a terminal REPL and the FluCoMa toolkit, it lets you load, analyse, slice, and play audio entirely from the keyboard — with inline waveform visualisations rendered right in the terminal.

This first post walks through the motivation behind the project, the technology choices, and where we're headed next.
